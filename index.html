No Fear

I hope I never forget that feeling of thankfulness as I looked into this girl’s eyes today while she was so passionately talking about life without fear because of Jesus. We were just in 6th & 7th grade yesterday losing our marbles over things written in Seventeen magazines. No other way could I have asked for our friendship to blossom. It only gets sweeter from here on out.

The Best Devotional

The best devotional book I’ve come across is Jesus Calling by Sarah Young. It’s interesting because it is written in the voice of Jesus talking to us, not just writing about Jesus. It’s so short that you really can’t miss it and it’s such a great perspective setter. I don’t actually own it because I just don’t want to buy the book, but I’ve borrowed it from a few of my friends for lengths at a time...
