About Me

Hi I'm Carol

I’m from New Jersey— grew up and graduated high school from the “shore”. Everything from my 1994 - 2012 requires a coffee date or sleepover to explain, but in short I’d always been a wreck. Somewhere along the lines I found myself at the feet of my Father and knew things were never going to be the same.

Moved out from my parents’ and lived in North Jersey while going to school at Nyack College for Pastoral Ministry. I [barely] finished freshman year and took a 6 month break for YWAM doing the Awaken DTS. Made some of the best memories with Jesus in my life! Click here to read the journey. During that time, God birthed and began to bloom the dreams of a cafè ministry in me. So now, I’m back at Nyack College as a Business Administration major, preparing and making myself available to the Lord to see these wild dreams pan out into a Kingdom cafè someday! I also found out during YWAM that I am absolutely not the introvert I thought I was, but I am wild and love people!

My plan for the future is whatever God places in front of me next because my life up until now is a testimony of the uselessness of planning everything out on my own.I have this blog because my hand aches too much trying to hand-write even a couple pages in my Moleskine everyday. I’m also long-winded so you have to be courageous to commit to my blog! My goal is simply to be a woman of integrity before the face of God and behind the eyes of man.

I’m on a painful, exciting, dreadful, refreshing, never-ending journey of sanctification. Welcome!
